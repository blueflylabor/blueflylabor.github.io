The Invention of Blotting Paper

Blotting paper was invented by accident. In the late 19th century, an American paper manufacturer, Charles Stratmann, was trying to make a stronger type of paper. However, due to a mistake in the materials, he ended up with a soft paper that had great absorbency. Coincidentally, he discovered that this paper could absorb oil very well. After some improvements, it became the blotting paper we use today, especially in the kitchen for cleaning oil.What started as a failed experiment turned into an unexpected innovation that’s still widely used today.

Advantage of Chemistry

Chemistry is extremely useful in everyday life. It helps us understand how different substances interact, which is crucial for things like cooking, cleaning, and medicine. For example, chemistry is behind the development of medicines and vaccines. It also explains natural phenomena, like how rust forms or why the weather changes. I love chemistry because it’s everywhere around us and affects so many aspects of our daily lives.
