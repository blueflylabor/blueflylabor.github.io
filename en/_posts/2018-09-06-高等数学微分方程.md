# Differential Equations

### I. Basic Concepts of Ordinary Differential Equations

##### 1. Differential Equation
$$
y'=2x
$$
Derivatives or differentials of equations containing an **unknown function**

##### 2. Order of differential equation
$$
1阶方程
$$
The **highest order derivative of the unknown function** that appears in a differential equation

##### 3. Solution to a Differential Equation
$$
y=f(x)=x^2
$$
**Functions satisfying differential equations**

##### 4. General solution of a differential equation
$$
y=f(x)=x^2+c
$$
If the solution of a differential equation contains **any constants**, and the **number of such constants** is equal to the order of the differential equation,

##### 5. Particular solutions of differential equations
$$
y=f(x)=x^2+1
$$
Differential equations **with solutions that do not contain any constants**

##### 6. Initial conditions

Determining a set of constants for the particular solution

##### 7. Integral curves

A solution to the equation corresponds to a curve in the plane

### II, First-order differential equations y' = f(x,y)

##### 1. Equations that can be *separated* into variables
$$
\begin{align}
&y'=f(x)g(y)\Leftrightarrow \frac{dy}{dx}=f(x)g(y)\Leftrightarrow \frac{dy}{g(y)}=f(x)dx \\
&求解方法对两端积分\Leftrightarrow \int\frac{dy}{g(y)}=\int f(x)dx\\
\end{align}
$$
##### 2. *Homogeneous* Differential Equations
$$
\begin{align}
&\frac{dy}{dx}=\Phi(\frac{y}{x})\\
&方法：令u=\frac{y}{x},y=ux,y'=u+u'x\\
\end{align}
$$
###### Example Problem

![1321sdads](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/blueflylabor/images/main/1321sdads.jpg)
$$
\begin{align}
&y'+\frac{y}{x}=(\frac{y}{x})^2\\
&令u=\frac{y}{x},y=ux,y'=u+u'x\\
&\Leftrightarrow u+u'x+u=u^2\\
&\Leftrightarrow u+\frac{du}{dx}x+u=u^2\\
&\Leftrightarrow \frac{du}{u^2-2u}=\frac{1}{x}dx\\
&\Leftrightarrow \int\frac{du}{u^2-2u}=\int\frac{1}{x}dx\\
&\Leftrightarrow \frac{1}{2}(\ln|u-2|-\ln|u|)=\ln|x|+C\\
&\Leftrightarrow \frac{u-2}{u}=Cx^2\\
\end{align}
$$
##### 3. First-order *linear* differential equations
$$
\begin{align}
&形如y'+p(x)y=Q(x)\\
&通解：y=e^{-\int p(x)dx}[\int Q(x)e^{\int p(x)dx}+C]\\
\end{align}
$$
##### 4. Bernoulli Equation
$$
\begin{align}
&形如y'+p(x)y=Q(x)y^n(n \neq 0,1)\\
&方法：令u=y^{1-n}\\
&y^{-\alpha}y'+p(x)y^{1-\alpha}=Q(x)\\
&令u=y^{1-\alpha}\\
&(1-\alpha)y^{-\alpha}y'=\frac{du}{dx}\\
\end{align}
$$
![img](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/blueflylabor/images/main/0P50G64~_SZYBCKV~_$$WHJG.jpg)
$$
\begin{align}
&观察到微分方程中包含y''和y'和y,该方程为y''=f(y,y')型\\
&令y'=p,y''=p\frac{dp}{dy}\\
&得yp\frac{dp}{dy}+p^2=0\\
&\Leftrightarrow y\frac{dp}{dy}+p=0\\
&当p=0时,该方程成立,但跟据初始条件y|_{x=0}=1\\
&即p=1与其不符\\
&\Leftrightarrow \int\frac{dp}{p}=-\int\frac{dy}{y}\\
&\Leftrightarrow |py|=e^c\\
&\Leftrightarrow p=\frac{c}{y}\\
&\Leftrightarrow \frac{dy}{dx}=\frac{c}{y}\\
&根据初始条件y'|_{x=0}=\frac{1}{2}\Leftrightarrow c=\frac{1}{2}\\
&\Leftrightarrow y^2=x+c\\
&跟据初始条件y|_{x=0}=1,y^2=x+1\\
&又y|_{x=0}=10,y=\sqrt{x+1}
\end{align}
$$

&a'=1,b'=2\\
&则非齐次方程的通解为y=(C_1+C_2x)e^x+x+2\\
